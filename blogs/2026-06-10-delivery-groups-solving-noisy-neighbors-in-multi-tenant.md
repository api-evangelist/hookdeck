Webhook traffic can be bursty and uneven, and in any system processing webhooks for multiple tenants (multi-store eCommerce apps, payment integrations, SaaS or fintech connectors) one tenant's burst becomes everyone's backlog. Hookdeck's new Delivery Groups feature isolates per-tenant delivery to prevent these noisy-neighbor backlogs in multi-tenant webhook delivery.
